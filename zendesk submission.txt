View related Brushfire attendees, orders, and groups alongside tickets

=======================

With this app installed, you're a single click away from searching all across Brushfire for attendees, orders, groups, or customer accounts that match the email address of the owner of the currently visible ticket.

=======================

To Install the Brushfire app:

1. Sign into your Zendesk, then click the Admin icon (the gear) in the sidebar.
2. Select Apps > Browse, then find the Brushfire app and click the tile.
3. On the Brushfire app page, click **Install app** in the upper-right corner.
4. Enter a descriptive name for your app, then click **Install.**
5. Provide your API Key in Brushfire > App Configuration > **Brushfire API Key**.

Note: To get an API Key, please request one using the form at https://developer.brushfire.com/key and specify that you are setting up your Zendesk integration.
